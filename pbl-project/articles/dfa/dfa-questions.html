General Tips for DFA Construction

  • Start by identifying the patterns you need to track
  • Think about what information needs to be remembered in states
  • Consider edge cases and boundary conditions
  • Test your DFA with both accepting and rejecting strings
  • Make sure all states have transitions for all input symbols
