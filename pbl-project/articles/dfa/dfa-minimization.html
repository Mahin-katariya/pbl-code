DFA Minimization: Making Your DFA Simpler

Medium 12 min read Last updated: March 2024

üéØ Why Minimize?

Think of DFA minimization like decluttering your room - we remove states we never use (unreachable states) and combine states that do the same thing (equivalent states). A simpler DFA is easier to understand and more efficient to implement!

How to Minimize a DFA

Two Main Steps:

  1. Remove Unreachable States

    States we can never get to from the start state

  2. Merge Equivalent States

    States that behave exactly the same way

Quick Summary

DFA minimization is like spring cleaning - remove what you can't reach (unreachable states) and combine what's duplicate (equivalent states). The result is a simpler, more efficient DFA that does exactly the same job!
